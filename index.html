About Me

Hello! I live in Barrington, NH. When I am not working at Liberty Mutual, I read a lot, run a lot and talk way too much about my kids. My husband Phil and I have been together for 31 years and married for 22. Our daughter Stephanie is a junior at Purdue University (Boiler Up!) and is studying to be an environmental engineer. She is outspoken and passionate about the things she loves. Our son Cameron is a senior at Oyster River High School (go Bobcats!). He is an athlete and a theater kid. He hopes to play varsity baseball and be in the school musical this spring. I will try not to bore my fellow Boot Campers too much with stories about them.

I am currently training to run the Chicago Marathon in October. This will be my fifth marathon. Other marathons I have run include:

  • Seacoast Rockfest (2010)
  • Clarence de Mar in Keene, NH (2013)
  • Boston Marathon (2015)
  • Marine Corps Marathon in DC (2017)

Other favorite races include Reach the Beach, Disney Princess Half Marathon, the Great Island 5k and the Wine and Dine 10k and Half Marathon races at Disney.
